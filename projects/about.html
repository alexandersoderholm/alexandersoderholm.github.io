About

I’m Alexander, an architecture student based in Lund. My work explores how buildings can support everyday life — socially, spatially, and over time. I’m especially interested in adaptive reuse and social infrastructure, where design becomes a tool for strengthening communities.

I approach each project with curiosity, precision, and hands‑on making — from digital modelling to fabrication. What motivates me is creating architecture that is generous, practical, and open to the people who use it.

I’m currently studying at Lund University, with a Bachelor’s degree from Umeå School of Architecture.
