About me

I'm Alexander, an architecture student based in Lund. My work focuses on how buildings can support everyday life — socially, spatially, and over time. I'm especially interested in adaptive reuse and social infrastructure, where design becomes a tool for strengthening communities. I approach each project with a mix of curiosity, precision, and hands-on making, from digital modelling to fabrication. What motivates me is creating architecture that is generous, practical, and open to the people who use it.
